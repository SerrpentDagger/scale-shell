Feathers and Flame
A scented candle and a swish of wings
Perched quietly on the rooftop
A wisp of smoke
Smoldering gently on the table
Its silence startling
Cozy in the flickering light
Claws scratching in the bark
Shifting shadows on the wall
A twitch in the darkness
A pair of eyes, reflecting
Drops of wax on the windowsill
The sizzle and spark of life
The freedom of flight; to escape
A pair of lamps shine the way
Calling to you from afar
Over the fences, through the windows
You choose your path
Take comfort in the unseen
Under cover
